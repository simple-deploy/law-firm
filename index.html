What We Do

Specialist Legal Services
Across All Areas of Family &
Property Law

We offer comprehensive, accredited legal services for individuals, families, and couples navigating Australia's family court system.

01
Family Law & Divorce
Most Popular
Separation Divorce Orders De Facto Relationships Mediation
Navigating separation is emotionally and legally complex. We provide clear, strategic advice from the first meeting to final orders — covering marriage dissolution, de facto separations, and all interim arrangements. We prioritise out-of-court resolution wherever possible to save you time, cost, and distress.
02
Property Settlements
Complex Cases Welcome
Asset Division Superannuation Splitting Business Interests Interstate Property
Australian property law requires careful valuation, negotiation, and court approval. Our specialists handle all asset classes — residential property, investment portfolios, self-managed super funds, family businesses, and offshore assets — ensuring you receive a fair and legally binding outcome.
03
Binding Financial Agreements
Prenups & Post-Nups
Prenuptial Agreements Post-Separation BFAs Cohabitation Agreements
A properly drafted Binding Financial Agreement (BFA) under the Family Law Act 1975 can protect your assets before marriage, during a relationship, or after separation. Our accredited specialists ensure your agreement is watertight and enforceable in any Australian court.
04
Child Custody & Parenting Plans
Child-First Approach
Parenting Orders Child Support Relocation Applications International Parenting
We help parents establish clear, workable parenting arrangements that protect their children's wellbeing. From consent orders and parenting plans to contested custody hearings, we advocate firmly for outcomes that reflect the best interests of every child.
05
Domestic Violence & AVOs
Urgent Assistance Available
Apprehended Violence Orders Emergency Orders Safety Planning
Safety is paramount. We provide urgent representation for Apprehended Domestic Violence Orders (ADVOs), emergency protection orders, and safe exit planning. Our team can assist after hours and on weekends for immediate risk situations.
06
Wills & Estate Planning
Plan Ahead
Wills Powers of Attorney Guardianship Orders Probate
Protect your family's future with a comprehensive estate plan. We draft legally robust wills, enduring powers of attorney, advance care directives, and administer estates through probate — giving your loved ones clarity and security.
